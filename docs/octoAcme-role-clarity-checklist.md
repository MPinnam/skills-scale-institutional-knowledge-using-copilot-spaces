# OctoAcme Role Clarity Checklist

Use this checklist at project kickoff or team onboarding to ensure roles and responsibilities are clearly defined and understood.

- [ ] All relevant personas are assigned (Product, Project, Developers, UX, Scrum Master, Business Analyst, DevOps, QA)
- [ ] Role summaries and responsibilities reviewed by all team members
- [ ] Handoffs and collaboration points between roles are documented
- [ ] Project board owners and communication channels are specified
- [ ] Role-based documentation (process, checklists, templates) is accessible
