## Overview of OctoAcme Project Management Processes

OctoAcme’s project management approach is grounded in these core principles:

- **Customer-first**: Prioritize customer value and usability in all work.
- **Iterative delivery**: Ship small, testable increments instead of large bets.
- **Clear roles**: Defined responsibilities for Project Managers (PM), Product Managers (PdM), Developers, QA, and Stakeholders.
- **Data-informed decisions**: Use metrics and evidence to guide improvements and product direction.
- **Psychological safety**: Encourage honest feedback, experimentation, and learning from failures.
- **Consistent, repeatable workflows**:
    - Initiation and chartering
    - Planning and backlog creation
    - Execution & tracking
    - Release & deployment
    - Retrospectives and continuous improvement
    - Risk and communication management
    - Clear documentation and accessible knowledge

> These processes improve onboarding, collaboration, and quality by providing all team members with a single, central entry point for project knowledge.
